How to play

The face on top will pull a ridiculous expression — a careta.

When the timer starts, you have 6 seconds to copy it with your own face in the camera.

At the flash, your face is frozen and scored from 0 to 100. 100 is a perfect mimic!

Your camera stays on your device — nothing is uploaded, ever.
